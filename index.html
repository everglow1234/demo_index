Purpose: Step-by-step calculation of Equiverse Score for a sample company

STEP 1: RAW DATA FROM BRSR REPORT

Employee Distribution by Tier and Gender

Additional BRSR Data

  • Female turnover last year: 320 employees
  • Total females (start of year): 3,100
  • Employees who took parental leave: 150
  • Employees who returned from parental leave: 120

STEP 2: CALCULATE PRIMARY VARIABLES

Variables V1-V5: Female Ratio at Each Tier

Variable Formula Calculation Result
R1 (Entry) F1 / T1 2,000 / 5,000 0.400 (40.0%)
R2 (Junior) F2 / T2 700 / 2,000 0.350 (35.0%)
R3 (Middle) F3 / T3 200 / 800 0.250 (25.0%)
R4 (Senior) F4 / T4 40 / 200 0.200 (20.0%)
R5 (C-Suite) F5 / T5 3 / 20 0.150 (15.0%)

Variable V6: Pipeline Flow Ratio (PFR)

PFR = R5 / R1 = 0.150 / 0.400 = 0.375
Interpretation: Only 37.5% of entry-level female representation flows through to C-Suite. Significant leakage!

Variable V7: Female Retention Rate (RET_F)

RET_F = 1 - (Female Turnover / Total Females at Start)
RET_F = 1 - (320 / 3,100) = 1 - 0.103 = 0.897 (89.7%)

Variable V8: Parental Leave Return Rate (PLR)

PLR = Returned / Took Leave = 120 / 150 = 0.800 (80.0%)

STEP 3: CALCULATE DERIVED VARIABLES

D1: Drop-off Rate (Entry → Middle)

PDR_1-3 = (R1 - R3) / R1 = (0.400 - 0.250) / 0.400 = 0.375 (37.5% drop)

D2: Drop-off Rate (Middle → C-Suite)

PDR_3-5 = (R3 - R5) / R3 = (0.250 - 0.150) / 0.250 = 0.400 (40.0% drop)

Bottleneck Identification

Transition Drop-off Rate Status
Entry → Junior 12.5% Normal
Junior → Middle 28.6% 🚨 "Broken Rung"
Middle → Senior 20.0% High
Senior → C-Suite 25.0% High
BOTTLENECK TIER = Junior → Middle Management (28.6% drop)

STEP 4: CALCULATE DIMENSION SCORES

A. Representation Score (S_Rep)

Formula: S_Rep = Σ(wi × Ri)

Tier Weight (wi) Ratio (Ri) wi × Ri
Entry (w1) 0.05 0.400 0.020
Junior (w2) 0.10 0.350 0.035
Middle (w3) 0.15 0.250 0.0375
Senior (w4) 0.20 0.200 0.040
C-Suite (w5) 0.50 0.150 0.075
S_Rep = 0.2075
Normalized: S_Rep = 0.2075 / 0.50 = 0.415 (41.5%)

B. Progression Score (S_Prog)

S_Prog = 0.50 × PFR + 0.25 × (1 - PDR_1-3) + 0.25 × (1 - PDR_3-5)

S_Prog = 0.50 × 0.375 + 0.25 × (1 - 0.375) + 0.25 × (1 - 0.400)
S_Prog = 0.1875 + 0.15625 + 0.150
S_Prog = 0.494 (49.4%)

C. Retention Score (S_Ret)

S_Ret = 0.60 × RET_F + 0.40 × PLR

S_Ret = 0.60 × 0.897 + 0.40 × 0.800
S_Ret = 0.5382 + 0.320
S_Ret = 0.858 (85.8%)

STEP 5: CALCULATE FINAL EQUIVERSE SCORE

EQUIVERSE SCORE = 0.50 × S_Rep + 0.30 × S_Prog + 0.20 × S_Ret
Dimension Score Weight Contribution
Representation 0.415 0.50 0.2075
Progression 0.494 0.30 0.1482
Retention 0.858 0.20 0.1716
EQUIVERSE SCORE = 0.5273

52.73 / 100

Rating: AVERAGE

STEP 8: ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS

Priority 1: Fix the Broken Rung (Junior → Middle)

Problem: 28.6% of women drop off between Junior and Middle Management

Actions:

  • Review promotion criteria for Middle Management roles
  • Implement sponsorship programs for high-potential women at Junior level
  • Audit performance review processes for gender bias
  • Create visible pathways and success stories

Priority 2: Accelerate Senior → C-Suite Pipeline

Problem: Only 15% women at C-Suite vs 20% at Senior

Actions:

  • Board diversity mandate (target 30% women)
  • Succession planning with gender lens
  • Executive coaching for senior women
  • External hiring of women for C-Suite roles

Priority 3: Maintain Retention Strength

Current: 89.7% retention rate (strong)

Actions:

  • Document what's working and replicate
  • Monitor for any deterioration
  • Improve parental leave return rate from 80% to 90%+
